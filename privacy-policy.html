Privacy Policy

Singularitya was founded with the purpose of making the use of artificial intelligence simpler, more accessible, and more efficient for people and organizations.

Singularitya values the privacy and protection of personal data of its customers, collaborators, partners, suppliers, and other data subjects. We are committed to adopting the best information security practices, fully respecting applicable legislation, especially Law No. 13,709/2018 - the General Data Protection Law (LGPD).

This Privacy Policy aims to inform our users, partners, and collaborators about how their personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected when using our services or when interacting with the Company by any means.

If you have questions, comments, or requests related to this Policy or the processing of your personal data, please contact us:

contact@singularitya.com

HOW DO WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

We collect your personal data in different ways, always with the aim of offering a more efficient, secure, and personalized experience in our services. The main forms of collection are:

  • Information provided by you: When you start a conversation with our chatbots on WhatsApp, request information about our solutions, participate in events, or contact us directly by phone, email, or social media.
  • Receiving data from third parties: We may receive your personal data through recruitment companies, referrals, or other sources that share your data with us for specific purposes, such as selection processes.
  • Memorization of contextual aspects of the conversation: To offer a more natural experience with our AI agents, we may memorize information such as name, preferences, last request, and recent history, respecting the principles of the LGPD.

WHY DO WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

We process your personal data to:

  • Register and identify users;
  • Operate our chatbots and intelligent agents;
  • Charge for contracted services;
  • Communicate with you via official channels;
  • Marketing and promotion of our services;
  • Contact leads and potential customers;
  • Usage analysis for improvements;
  • Security of interactions and systems;
  • Compliance with legal or regulatory obligations;
  • Recruitment and selection;
  • Events and research;
  • Audits, compliance, and legal defense;
  • Integration with platforms such as WhatsApp Business, Google Ads, Meta Ads.

WHAT DATA DO WE COLLECT?

  • Registration: name, phone, email, position, company, CPF/CNPJ.
  • Chatbot interaction: messages, commands, preferences, times.
  • Conversation context: recent history, name used, approximate location.
  • Device and navigation: IP, browser, OS, session duration.
  • Payments: billing and invoicing data.
  • Customer service and support: contact records.
  • Recruitment: resume, professional networks, email, phone.
  • Other voluntary data entered in forms, events, and surveys.
