About Us

SingularityA was founded by Geraldo Leite, the company's CEO and lead developer. He holds degrees in Geography and Statistics, a Master's in Information Systems, and is currently pursuing a PhD in Cognitive Science and Language. With over 5 years of experience as a data scientist, Geraldo leads the development of intelligent assistants focused on productivity, natural language, and integration with platforms like WhatsApp and Telegram.

At SingularityA, we aim to integrate insights from human cognition into our agents, exploring mechanisms such as memory to make interactions more natural, contextual, and personalized.

Contact: contact@singularitya.com LinkedIn: Geraldo Leite

Our Mission

We specialize in transforming WhatsApp and Telegram into productivity tools powered by artificial intelligence. We enable individuals and businesses to create configurable virtual assistants without the need for external platforms or technical knowledge. Our goal is to integrate AI into daily life, fostering efficient collaboration between humans and technology.
